Chart page

This is the main moving map page.

  • Backgroud is the moving map.
  • Select which moving map by clicking the Chart button or the black triangle button in the lower right corner. You will be presented with a list of charts that are applicable to the lat/lon of the screen.
  • Upper left corner shows the latest position received by the GPS. It can be hidden by clicking on it and it will shrink to a red triangle button. The latest GPS position is indicated by the red airplane icon on the map, provided the display contains the lat/lon of the latest GPS position. Click the red triangle button to have it displayed again.
  • Lower left corner shows the lat/lon position of the center of the display. The center will be identified by a blue cross in the center. The numerics may be hidden by clicking on the numbers and they will shrink into a blue triangle button and the cross will be removed from the display. Click the blue triangle button to have it displayed again.
  • If a course is selected, the course information will be shown in the upper right corner in green along with a green course line. The course information and line may be hidden by clicking on the numbers and it will shrink to a triangle button and the course line will be remove from the display. Click the green triangle button to have it displayed again.
  • The gray triangle button in the middle of the bottom edge of the display will hide/show the page selection buttons along the bottom.

The details of what appears in the lower left corner may vary based on the current situation. At most, lines such as these will appear:



              6.79 nm
              011° Mag
            42°41.4634'N
            70°53.3888'W
                 x 1
              015° Mag
  • 6.79 nm - distance from airplane icon to center of screen. Not shown if aircraft is centered on screen.
  • 011° Mag - heading rom airplane icon to center of screen. Not shown if aircraft is centered on screen.
  • 42°41.4634'N - latitude of center of screen. Not shown if aircraft is centered on screen.
  • 70°53.3888'W - longitude f center of screen. Not shown if aircraft is centered on screen.
  • x 1 - chart magnification factor. x1 indicates standard 500,000:1 sectional magnification.
  • 015° Mag - chart chart rotation factor, ie, what chart heading is 'UP'.

NOTE: If you manually pan the display, the chart will fix in place and will not track movement of the airplane. The airplane will move but the chart will not. To re-engage tracking, click the Android Menu button then click Re-center. The map will move to place the airplane in the center of the display, the blue cross will be taken away and the information in the lower left corner will be reduced to a minimum.
