Crumbs page

This page manages breadcrumbs trails of your flights.

  • Create... - creates a new breadcrumb trail beginning with the next received GPS position.
  • Previously recorded breadcrumbs trails appear as buttons below the Create... button. Clicking these buttons will cause the corresponding trail to be re-drawn on the map and the beginning of the trail will appear at the center of the screen.
  • You can view the crumbs files under the Files button, then click on crumbs.
